Marketing Campaign Process & Schedule

Campaign Workflow Overview

  • Initial Setup: Start with pixel setup and integration with Stripe to measure sales.
  • Research & Persona Building: Conduct marketing research to build the Buyer Persona.
  • Creative Development: Create ad copies and creatives tailored to the Buyer Persona's problems, frustrations, and goals.
  • Approval & Launch: Once creatives are approved, the ads go live.
  • Initial Monitoring (7 Days): Allow 7 days for ads and audiences to spend the budget and monitor their initial performance.
  • First Optimization: After 7 days, begin optimizing by deactivating low-performing ads to reallocate budget to top performers.
  • Audience Management: If an audience shows no well-performing ads, it will be deactivated and replaced with a new one.
  • Saturation & Refresh Cycle (1-2 Months): Typically, after 1-2 months, ads may become saturated. At this point, refresh audiences with new creatives and restart the optimization process.
